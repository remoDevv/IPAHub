Tutorials

How to Install IPA Files

  1. Download the IPA file from our library
  2. Install a third-party app installer like AltStore or Sideloadly on your computer
  3. Connect your iOS device to your computer
  4. Use the app installer to install the IPA file on your device
  5. Trust the developer profile in your device settings
  6. Enjoy your newly installed app!

How to Upload IPA Files

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on the "Upload" button in the navigation menu
  3. Fill in the required information about your app
  4. Upload the IPA file and an app icon
  5. Submit your app for review
  6. Wait for approval from our admin team

Troubleshooting

  • App won't install

    Make sure you're using the latest version of your chosen app installer and that your device is compatible with the IPA file.

  • App crashes on launch

    Try reinstalling the app or check if there's an updated version available in our library.

  • Upload fails

    Ensure your IPA file is not corrupted and that it meets our size requirements. If issues persist, contact our support team.
